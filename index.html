How scoring works

🛣️
Minimize total miles400 pts — Every extra mile costs points proportionally.
Formula: 400 × (optimal miles ÷ your miles)
At optimal → 400 pts  ·  5% over → ~381 pts  ·  10% over → ~364 pts  ·  25% over → 320 pts
🗺️
Route match vs system300 pts — How closely your store groupings match the optimal solution. Each of the 25 orders is worth 12 pts.
Formula: 300 × (matched orders ÷ 25)
25/25 matched → 300 pts  ·  20/25 → 240 pts  ·  15/25 → 180 pts
🕐
Delivery window compliance150 pts — Every store has a delivery window shown on its order chip. All stops on a truck must have compatible windows — your driver needs to reach each store while it’s open.
Formula: 150 × (stores reached within window ÷ total stores)
💡 Tip: Window compatibility is the main reason certain stores are paired together. If two stores on the same truck have windows that don’t overlap enough to serve both, you’ll lose points.
📦
Assign all orders100 pts — Every order placed on a truck earns its share. Each of the 25 orders is worth 4 pts.
Formula: 100 × (assigned orders ÷ 25)
25/25 assigned → 100 pts  ·  24/25 → 96 pts  ·  20/25 → 80 pts
⚡
Speed bonus50 pts — Full points if you finish with 4+ minutes left. Points drop linearly from there down to 0 at time's up.
